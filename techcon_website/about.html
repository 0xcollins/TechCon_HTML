The Evolution Of Techcon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a major international event, attracting thousands of attendees from all corners of the globe. From its humble beginnings in a community hall to filling large convention centers, TechCon has continuously evolved to meet the changing landscape of technology and innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education within the technology community. Past Speakers

Name: Mark Essien

Biography: Mark Essien is a Nigerian entrepreneur, software developer, and founder of Hotels.ng, one of the largest hotel booking websites in Nigeria. Mark is also a prominent advocate for tech innovation in Africa. He has invested in various startups and has been actively involved in mentoring young tech entrepreneurs. His efforts have significantly contributed to the growth of the tech ecosystem in Nigeria, making him a key figure in the African technology landscape.

Name: Yele Bademosi

Biography: Yele Bademosi is a Nigerian entrepreneur and venture capitalist known for his contributions to the African tech and blockchain ecosystems. He is the founder of Bundle, a social payment app for cash and cryptocurrencies, and a founding partner at Microtraction, a venture capital firm that invests in African startups at the earliest stages.
